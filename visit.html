Why This Exists

Why Expedition PH

The Philippines is one of the most biodiverse places on Earth — and one of the least known outside its borders. This site is a personal effort to document, share, and celebrate the natural heritage of the archipelago: its species, its landscapes, its geological history, and the conservation challenges it faces.

All content here is compiled from published scientific literature, field observations, and a deep personal passion for Filipino wildlife and wild places.
