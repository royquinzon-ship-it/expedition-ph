◆   Flora   ◆

9,253 Vascular Plant Species

Philippine Flora

More than 3,500 plant species found nowhere else on Earth — from ancient dipterocarp forests to carnivorous highland pitcher plants.

Endemic Tree
Red LauanShorea polysperma

Once the dominant tree of Philippine lowland forests — now critically threatened by decades of industrial logging.

Critically Endangered
Orchid
Waling-Waling

The Queen of Philippine Orchids, blooming in the highlands of Mindanao.

Carnivorous Plant
Attenborough's Pitcher PlantNepenthes attenboroughii

Discovered in 2009 on a single mountain in Palawan — one of the largest carnivorous plants on Earth, capable of digesting rats. Named after Sir David Attenborough.

Critically Endangered
Parasitic Flower
Giant Mindanao RafflesiaRafflesia schadenbergiana

The largest flower endemic to the Philippines — a rootless, leafless parasite that lives invisibly inside a vine, emerging only to bloom for 5–7 days before rotting.

Critically Endangered

Philippine Fauna

Among the world's most threatened biodiversity hotspots — extraordinary creatures fighting for survival.

Raptor
Philippine EaglePithecophaga jefferyi

The national bird and world's most powerful raptor. Fewer than 800 remain.

Critically Endangered
Primate
Philippine TarsierCarlito syrichta

One of the world's smallest primates with eyes larger than its brain.

Endemic Mammal
TamarawBubalus mindorensis

A small solitary buffalo found only on Mindoro. Fewer than 600 survive.
